FAQs



You can find carpool matches by using our platform's search feature, which matches you with drivers or passengers based on your location, destination, and preferred travel times.

We use OneCard as our payment system, and riders can enjoy free rides until they reach a $15 limit. Once they hit the limit, they need to pay to request additional rides.

In the event of a driver canceling the carpool at the last minute, we provide alternative arrangements or refunds to affected passengers.

If you experience a delay or need to change your travel plans while carpooling, please communicate promptly with your fellow carpoolers to coordinate any necessary adjustments.

We prioritize safety and conduct thorough driver screenings, including background checks, to ensure a safe carpooling experience.

You can discuss luggage or equipment arrangements with your carpool group, as allowances may vary depending on the space available in the vehicle.
